WHAT IS HANIWA?

Haniwa is a group of 5 music makers based out of Oklahoma City. Shirley Sanders (vocals, guitar, and more), Dylan Walling (vocals, keys, and more), Tyco Holloway (vocals, bass, and more), Shawn Stafford (guitar), and Jake Jones (drums).

HANIWA IS MUSIC?

Heck Yeah! The band's debut self-titled album was released during the fall of 2014 and is available on many major streaming services such as iTunes, Spotify, and even this very website! The band has just finished making their 2nd album, "Violent Sun" , and is nearing its release date. We hope to see you at The Opolis August 18th! ;)

HOW DO YOU PRONOUNCE HANIWA?

HA like HAllway. NI like "kNEE". WA like "WAter". All together now, "HA-NI-WA"! Now you're in the know, cool kid.
